(the <font style='font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman'>&#8220;</font>Company<font style='font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman'>&#8221;</font>) held a 2020 Special Meeting of Stockholders (the &#147;Special Meeting&#148;).
