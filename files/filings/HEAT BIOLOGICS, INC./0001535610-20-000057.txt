As calculated in accordance with Rule 13d-3 of the Securities Exchange
Act of 1934, as amended, (i) Sabby Volatility Master Fund, Ltd.
beneficially owns 4,300,000 shares of the Issuer's common stock (common
shares), representing approximately 7.49% of the Common Stock, and
(ii) Sabby Management, LLC and Hal Mintz each beneficially own
4,300,000 shares of the common shares, representing approximately 7.49%
of the common shares. Sabby Management, LLC and Hal Mintz do not
directly own any common shares, but each indirectly owns 4,300,000
common shares. Sabby Management,LLC, a Delaware limited liability
company, indirectly owns 4,300,000 common shares because it serves as
the investment manager of Sabby Volatility Warrant Master Fund, Ltd.,
a Cayman Islands company. Mr. Mintz indirectly owns 4,300,000 common
shares in his capacity as manager of Sabby Management,LLC.
